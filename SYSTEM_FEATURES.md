## Aesthetic Intelligence

### Aesthetic Consistency Scoring
Quantified measurement of visual/sonic/textual coherence across your work.

**Algorithm Components:**
- Color palette analysis
- Typography consistency
- Compositional patterns
- Tonal voice analysis
- Material language
- Spatial relationships

**Scoring Range:** 0-100
- 95-100: Exceptional consistency
- 85-94: Strong consistency
- 75-84: Good consistency
- Below 75: Review recommended

**Implementation Status:** Mock data in Fashion Lab

### Brand Drift Detection
Real-time alerts when creative work deviates from established aesthetic.

**Detection Methods:**
- Visual signature matching
- Tonal analysis
- Material deviation scoring
- Compositional variance
- Color palette drift
- Typography inconsistency

**Alert Levels:**
- **Minor Drift** (1-5%): Informational
- **Moderate Drift** (6-15%): Warning
- **Major Drift** (16%+): Block with override

**Implementation Status:** Scoring system active, alerts pending
